What Carter Lost

What Carter Lost

2018 / 67 min

In the fall of 1988 a Texas high school fielded the football team that many consider the greatest ever, yet chances are good you have never heard their story. What Carter Lost, directed by Adam Hootnick for ESPN Films’ 30 for 30 series, is the saga of that team, the Dallas Carter Cowboys. I was an archival producer for the project.
